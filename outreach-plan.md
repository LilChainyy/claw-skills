## Rules

1. One company per day — each one-pager takes real effort. Mass-applying one-pagers is worse than one great one.
2. DM is a door knock, not a pitch. 75 words max.
3. The one-pager does the work. If it's more than one page, cut it.
4. One follow-up only, 3-4 days later: "Following up on my note — happy to send anything else that'd be useful."
5. After follow-up: submit ATS form as backup. Move on.
6. Start with the company where you know the founder's name. Cold outreach to a named person converts 3x better than to a generic inbox.
